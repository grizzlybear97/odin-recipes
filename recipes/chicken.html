Chicken Recipe

This Indian-inspired creamy chicken curry recipe is similar to a curry I had in India.

The aromatic spices and flavors are a delight to the senses! Delicious with fresh naan and basmati rice.

How to make Chicken Curry Indian style

Ingredients

  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 1/2 ginger garlic paste
  • salt to taste
  • 1/2 kg chicken curry cut
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 1/2 lemon juiced
  • chillie power to taste

Directions

  1. Heat oil in pan at medium heat, saute onion until lightly brown.
  2. Stir in garlic paste and salt for 2 minutes.
  3. Add chicken pieces, yogurt and milk. Bring to boil, and simmer for 20 to 25 minutes.
  4. Stir in lemon juice and chilli power. Simmer for 5 minutes more.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy!
