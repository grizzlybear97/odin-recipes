Potato Curry

Description

This is potato curry, Sri Lankan-style. Sri Lankans usually eat this dish with plain rice and other vegetable curries.

Ingredients

  • potatoes
  • salt
  • coconut
  • curry leaves
  • saffron
  • water
  • garlic

Steps

  1. Combine the potatoes, salt, coconut cream, green chiles, curry leaves, cayenne pepper, saffron powder, garlic, and water in a saucepan.
  2. Cook over medium heat, stirring frequently, until the coconut cream thickens, about 15 to 20 minutes.
  3. Test the potatoes for doneness by piercing them with the tip of a paring knife. When the potatoes are tender, remove the pan from the heat. Let stand for 5 minutes before serving. Serve with plain rice.
